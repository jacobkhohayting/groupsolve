GroupSolve

GroupSolve is an educational program that supports students and educators with engaging classes, accessible resources, and high-quality learning materials.

M

Our Mission

Groupsolve exists to spark collaboration, break down barriers to information, and simplify the path forward. By focusing on discovery and exploration over memorization, we make it easy for every visitor to find their place, understand the goals, and immediately take their next step with us.

V

Our Vision

We imagine a dynamic ecosystem built for the future of learning where mathematical communication is seamless, goals are entirely transparent, and collective progress is visible to everyone. This platform is designed to keep that shared vision clear, accessible, and inspiring.

The Process

To reach our goal, we have three main tasks we aim to accomplish.

1

Classes

We want to develop a place where students can learn in a welcoming environment. Weekly, we hold sessions for students to constantly prepare and learn new mathematics. Anyone is invited as long as they want to learn!

2

Competitions

We will be holding numerous competitions throughout the school year. Students must have an opportunity to showcase their skills and see themselves improve, and we hope to be the place for that!

3

Coaches & Resources

Every school has a math club of some sort. We believe growth needs to happen daily. There is one thing that happens daily: school. We hope to provide schools with new resources and curriculum to help foster growth.
