Competitions

Friendly, well-designed competitions help students test ideas, learn from one another, and see growth in a new way.

Our philosophy

Compete, Reflect, Improve

A competition should be more than a ranking. We design events that make room for bold attempts, clear solutions, teamwork, and useful feedback after the final problem.

Designed differently

What Students Can Expect

1

Interesting problems

A range of entry points lets beginners begin while giving experienced students room to stretch.

2

Visible reasoning

Written explanations, team rounds, or solution talks reward communication—not only final answers.

3

Useful review

After the event, students receive solutions and opportunities to revisit the most instructive ideas.
